FitGlue ships 25 boosters that enrich, analyze and route your activities. Mix and match them in pipelines that fire on every workout.

METRICS · 8 boosters

COMPUTED FROM YOUR DATA
❤️

Heart Rate Summary

Min, avg, max BPM and HR drift. Detects dehydration and overreach early.

💗

Heart Rate Zones

Time in Z0–Z5 calculated from your LTHR. Pretty bar chart on every activity.

🔋

Calories Burned

Kcal expenditure with playful real-world equivalents ("≈ 1.0 slice of pizza 🍕").

💥

Effort Score

0–100 score blending HR, duration and intensity. Calibrates to your baseline.

💪

Training Load

TRIMP score with Recovery / Easy / Mod / Hard buckets so it's actually readable.

💤

Recovery Advisor

ACWR ratio + suggested recovery hours. Flags overreaching before you snap.

⏱️

Intervals

Auto-detects interval sets from power & HR. Builds a per-rep summary.

⛰️

Elevation Summary

Gain, loss and grade-adjusted pace. Now you know why that climb hurt.
