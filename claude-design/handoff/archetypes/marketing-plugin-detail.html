HEART RATE ZONES

Time spent in each training zone, calculated from your lactate threshold. The chart that tells you whether you actually did the work you said you did.

BOOSTER FREE TIER

Without zones, your HR data is just a wavy line. With them, every workout has a fingerprint — and a coach.

FitGlue's Heart Rate Zones booster calculates time in Z0–Z5 based on your lactate threshold heart rate (LTHR), then ships a clean zone breakdown to every destination that supports rich descriptions.

Features

  • Auto-calculates from LTHR or max HR — your choice
  • 5-zone or 7-zone model
  • Bar chart, emoji bar, or text-only formats
  • Per-destination format mapping
  • Weekly & monthly aggregates
  • Works with FIT, TCX, GPX and live webhook data

Perfect for

  • 🎯 Coaches checking athlete intent vs. execution
  • 🎯 Endurance athletes managing polarized training
  • 🎯 Anyone tired of "Morning Ride" with no context
