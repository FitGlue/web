## Overview

Parkrun is a free, weekly 5k event. FitGlue's Parkrun booster enriches your Parkrun activities with official times, position, and age grade from parkrun.org. To use it, you need to connect your Parkrun athlete ID.

## Setup

1. Find your **Parkrun athlete ID** (barcode number) — it's on your printed barcode or in the Parkrun app
2. Open **FitGlue Dashboard → Connections**
3. Click **Connect** on Parkrun
4. Enter your athlete ID

No password or OAuth — just your public athlete number.

## What It Enables

- [Parkrun booster](/help/articles/registry/enrichers/parkrun) — Official times, position, age grade, PB detection on your Strava parkruns
