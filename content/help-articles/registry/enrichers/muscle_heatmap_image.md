## Overview

The Muscle Heatmap Image booster creates an anatomical diagram showing which muscles you trained. Unlike the emoji/text Muscle Heatmap, this produces a visual SVG image — front and back body views with muscle groups highlighted by activation level.

## Tier

Muscle Heatmap Image requires the **Athlete** tier. It's not available on Hobbyist.

## Configuration

- **Coefficient Preset** — Standard, Powerlifting, or Bodybuilding
- **Anatomy Model** — Male or Female

## Best For

- Showcase pages — striking visual for shared activities
- Strength training from Hevy
- Users who want the full anatomical view
