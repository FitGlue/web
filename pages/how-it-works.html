Common questions.

How long does syncing take?
Most activities sync in under 30 seconds. The exact time depends on which boosters you've enabled — simple text boosters are instant, while AI features take a few seconds longer. Parkrun results sync automatically once official results are published (usually about 2 hours after your run).
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption for all connections — we never see your passwords. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you can disconnect any integration or delete your data at any time. See our security page for full details.
Can I use FitGlue with multiple devices?
Yes. FitGlue is device-agnostic. Whether your workout comes from your phone, a Garmin watch, a Fitbit, or a gym bike — if it reaches a connected source, FitGlue will process it. You can even have multiple sources feeding into the same pipeline.
What if a sync fails?
We've built robust retry mechanisms. If a sync fails (rare), we automatically retry. You can see the status of every activity in your dashboard, and failed syncs are clearly flagged so you know exactly what happened.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays yours. If you cancel your Athlete subscription, you'll drop to the free Hobbyist tier — your pipelines and history remain intact, you just lose access to premium features. If you delete your account, we permanently remove all your data from our servers.
