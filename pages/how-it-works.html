Common Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use OAuth for all connections, meaning we never see your passwords. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you can disconnect any integration at any time.

Does it work with all Hevy workouts?

Yes! Every workout you log in Hevy is automatically detected and processed. We convert the data to standard formats and enhance it with your chosen boosters.

Can I customize what gets synced?

You have full control. Create custom pipelines, choose specific boosters, and decide exactly where your data goes. It's your fitness journeyâ€”you're in charge.

What if a sync fails?

We've built robust retry mechanisms. If a sync fails (rare!), we automatically retry. You'll also get a notification so you're always in the loop.
