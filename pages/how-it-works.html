Common Questions

How long does syncing take?

Most activities sync in under 30 seconds. The exact time depends on which boosters you've enabled‚Äîsimple text boosters are instant, while AI features take a few seconds longer. Parkrun results sync automatically once the official results are published (usually about 2 hours after your run).

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all connections‚Äîwe never see your passwords. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you can disconnect any integration or delete your data at any time.

Can I use FitGlue with multiple devices?

Yes! FitGlue is device-agnostic. Whether your workout comes from your phone, a Garmin watch, a Fitbit, or a gym bike‚Äîif it reaches a connected source, FitGlue will process it. You can even have multiple sources feeding into the same pipeline.

Does it work with all Hevy workouts?

Yes! Every workout you log in Hevy is automatically detected and processed. We enhance it with your chosen boosters and send it wherever you want. Note: API access requires Hevy Pro subscription.

Can I customize what gets synced?

You have full control. Create custom pipelines, choose specific boosters, and decide exactly where your data goes. You can even use filters to only process certain activity types. It's your fitness journey‚Äîyou're in charge.

What if a sync fails?

We've built robust retry mechanisms. If a sync fails (rare!), we automatically retry. You can see the status of every activity in your dashboard, and failed syncs are clearly flagged so you know what happened.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data stays yours. If you cancel your Athlete subscription, you'll drop to the free Hobbyist tier‚Äîyour pipelines and history remain intact, you just lose access to premium features. If you delete your account, we permanently remove all your data from our servers.
