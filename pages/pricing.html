Frequently Asked Questions

Why open source?

We believe in transparency and community. Open source means you can audit the code, contribute improvements, and never worry about vendor lock-in.

What happens if I hit my sync limit?

Your syncs will pause until the next month. Upgrade to Athlete anytime for unlimited syncs—or self-host for free unlimited everything.

Can I try Athlete free first?

Yes! All new accounts get a 30-day Athlete trial. After that, you'll automatically switch to Hobbyist tier—no credit card required, no surprise charges.

What boosters are included in Hobbyist?

Almost everything! Workout summaries, muscle heatmaps, and stats are all available. Only the AI Companion (which requires significant compute) is exclusive to the Athlete tier.
