Questions answered.

How does the trial work?
Every new account starts with full Athlete access for 30 days. No credit card required upfront. After 30 days you'll drop to Hobbyist unless you subscribe — pipelines and data stay intact.
What happens if I hit my sync limit?
Your syncs pause until the next month. Upgrade to Athlete any time for unlimited syncs.
Which boosters are in Hobbyist?
Most boosters are free: workout summaries, emoji muscle heatmaps, heart rate overlays, parkrun results, weather, and all stats boosters. Athlete-exclusive features are AI-generated content (AI Companion, AI Banners) and premium visuals (Muscle Heatmap Images, Route Thumbnails).
Can I change my plan later?
Yes. Upgrade any time from your account settings. If you downgrade, you keep Athlete access until the end of your billing period, then switch to Hobbyist. No hassle, no hidden fees.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Your card details are handled securely by Stripe — we never see or store them.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period, then automatically switch to the free Hobbyist tier.
