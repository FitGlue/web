What is Showcase?

Showcase is FitGlue's built-in destination that creates beautiful, public activity pages for every workout you boost. Unlike Strava (which requires an account to view), Showcase pages are accessible to anyone with the link.

How to Enable Showcase

1

Add Showcase to Your Pipeline

When creating or editing a pipeline, add "Showcase" as one of your targets alongside Strava or other destinations.

2

Complete an Activity

Your next workout flows through the pipeline and FitGlue automatically generates a Showcase page.

3

Find Your Link

In your FitGlue dashboard, click on any activity to see the Showcase link. Or find it in your Activity history.

4

Share Anywhere

Copy the link and share it on social media, send it to friends, or embed it in your blog!
