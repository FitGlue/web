Frequently Asked Questions

Why open source?

We believe in transparency and community. Open source means you can audit the code, contribute improvements, and never worry about vendor lock-in.

What happens if I hit my sync limit?

Your syncs will pause until the next month. Upgrade to Pro anytime for unlimited syncs—or self-host for free unlimited everything.

Can I try Pro free first?

Yes! When we launch, Pro will have a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What boosters are included in Free?

Basic boosters like workout summaries and achievement badges. AI descriptions, muscle heatmaps, and advanced enrichments require Pro.
