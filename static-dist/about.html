About FitGlue

Our Mission

We believe your fitness data should work for you, not against you. Too many athletes and fitness enthusiasts spend precious time manually exporting, converting, and uploading their workout data between platforms.

FitGlue was born from frustration. Why should a Hevy strength workout require manual exports to appear on Strava? Why can't heart rate data from Fitbit automatically enrich your activities? Why is everything so... disconnected?

What We're Building

FitGlue is a fitness data orchestration platform. We connect to your favorite fitness apps, enhance your workout data with AI and additional metrics, and automatically sync everything to the platforms where you want to share your achievements.

Think of it as the glue that holds your fitness ecosystem together.
